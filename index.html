Vivek Gupta

 

Vivek Gupta
Research Fellow
Machine Learning, Natural Language Systems and Applications
Microsoft Research India

I am a research fellow in the Machine Learning, Natural Language Systems and Applications group at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Dr. Prateek Jain, Dr. Praneeth Netrapalli on problem related to machine learning and natural language processing. I also closely interact on ideas with postdoc Dr. Nagarajan Natarajan.

Previously, I was an undergraduate student at IIT Kanpur where I obtained my M. Tech and B. Tech (Dual Degree) in Computer Science in 2016. I worked on Heirarichal Product Classification and Text Document Representation with Prof. Harish Karnick for my master thesis. I have also closely worked with Prof Piyush Rai on problem of diversity priors for latent variable models and efficient algorithms for multi-label learning. I was also the first coordinator of Special Interest Group Machine Learning (SIGML) at IIT Kanpur.

I am generally interested in natural language processing and machine learning, speciallaly statistical and computational intricacy relevant to the use of machine learning. Mission: Make Machine Smarter !! Solve Intelligence. Uses the best technique in Machine Learning to build powerful general-purpose algorithms.
