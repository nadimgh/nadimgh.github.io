About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Toronto, hosted by Prof. Wei Yu. Prior to joining the University of Toronto, I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of California San Diego, where I was extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Young-Han Kim and Prof. Laurence B. Milstein. My research interests lie broadly in the intersections of information theory, wireless communication and signal processing.

Check out my CV for more details.
