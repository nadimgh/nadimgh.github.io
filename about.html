About Me

I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of British Columbia, hosted by Prof. Lele Wang.

From October 2023 to March 2025, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Toronto, hosted by Prof. Wei Yu.

I received a B.E. degree with honors in Computer and Communication Engineering from the American University of Beirut (AUB) in 2014, an M.S. degree in Communication Systems from the Swiss Federal Insitute of Technology of Lausanne (EPFL) in 2016 and a Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering (Communication Theory and Systems) from the University of California San Diego (UCSD) in 2022, where I was extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Young-Han Kim and Prof. Laurence B. Milstein. During my Ph.D., my research primarily focused on information theory and coding theory, with applications in communication, signal processing, computation and data compression.
